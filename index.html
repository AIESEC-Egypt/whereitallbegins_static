EXPERIENCE EGYPT

Egypt is called "where it all begins" due to its historical significance as one of the cradles of human civilization. Its ancient history, marked by the influential ancient Egyptian civilization, featured groundbreaking achievements in agriculture, architecture, mathematics, medicine, and writing.

Egypt's development of hieroglyphics, one of the world's earliest writing systems, allowed them to record their history and culture. These contributions to science, culture, and neighboring civilizations, such as the Greeks and Romans, earned Egypt its iconic reputation as the starting point of human civilization.
